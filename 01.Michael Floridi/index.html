HOW TO SUPPORT THEM ALONG THE JOURNEY

Absentee Operators do not want to put too much energy into the purchase process. They put a lot of trust into their supplier to offer guidance on the right solution for them – and reward it with loyalty if the entire process goes smoothly.

  • Value Proposition

    • Standard Solutions that make financial sense(ROI calculation, increased net income)
    • Trusted supplier that ensures a smooth process.

  • Support the process

    • Consultive Sellingadvising on solution fit, budget fit, internal decision process.
    • Check quote(clarity, details, consistency; focus esp on pricing explanation, lead times) and follow up quote.
    • Pro-actively communicate delivery and installation process(e.g expected delivery date, non-availability of products and parts
    • Once foot in the door, leverage good relationship to scale to other locations

  • Service Oppurtunity

    • Emphasize value-added services(remote monitoring, performance optimization) and operations consultation; consider contract model for equipment renewal and maintainance.
